Jakub Szewczyk

I am a post-doc at the Institute of Psychology at the Jagiellonian University in Kraków, Poland. Currently, I am funded by the Polonez Bis grant from the National Science Centre, Poland.
 
I study mechanisms and representations underlying language comprehension. I use EEG, fMRI, deep neural networks and behavioral measures.
 
Previously, I worked at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Donders Institute for Brain, Cognition and Behaviour, and Max Planck Institute for Psycholinguistics.
